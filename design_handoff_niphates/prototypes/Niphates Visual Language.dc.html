IV

The Dialogue

Chat is reframed as a transcript, not chat bubbles. Each turn is a named speaker with a struck rule — OPERATOR in mono, NIPHATES in reading serif. Spoken like a classical dialogue.

OPERATOR
What are the GPU temps on the rig right now?
NIPHATES
Both cards sit comfortably — GPU0 at 61°C, GPU1 at 58°C under the current Ollama load. Fans hold near 40%, nothing throttling. There is ample thermal headroom should you wish to summon a heavier model.
